Video Production

Football Culture Video Piece


This page shows two short videos for BBC's MOTDx. The first being a piece for the programme and the second being a clip for social media. In order to create this, we were put into groups. My group decided to focus on three different subtopics of football culture, those being, memorabilia, food and street art. We travelled to Classic Football Shirts in Deansgate to get opinions on how football shirts have evolved over the years and what people's favourite shirts are. We also went to the United Legends Cafe, as well as a piece of graffiti of Manchester United and England star, Marcus Rashford.
